Our History

Mike Boston and Tim Wilkison, the managing directors of Wilkison Partners, first met in 1990 in Charlotte, North Carolina, where they still reside. At the time, Mike was the General Manager of Olde Providence Racquet Club, one of the most successful tennis facilities in the US, and Tim was competing on the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) Tour. Both men saw in each other a drive to tackle and complete new projects and challenges at the highest level.

Their friendship developed, soon manifesting in a new company, Wilkison Partners. Over the past 10 years, Wilkison Partners has been involved in the planning and design of some of the country's finest master planned communities, acquiring a deep knowledge of the planning, marketing, financing, construction, and development of residential communities.

Wilkison Partners' guiding principle is to become the World's Finest Small Community Developer. By collaborating with developers and builders of the highest standard, Wilkison Partners provides great homeowner experiences in aesthetically pleasing and environmentally sustainable communities.
