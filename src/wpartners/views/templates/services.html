Services

Wilkison Partners has a proven track record of in each of the four functional areas that drive a development project success. Whether you are a potential development partner or a homeowner in one of our communities, we appreciate your interest in helping us become the World's Finest Small Community Developer.

Conception

We meet with the principles of the project to understand their objectives. Multiple aspects of the project are outlined and an initial timeline for the various stages of development is made.

Business Plan

We develop budgets and performance expectations around the project concept. A Profit/Loss statement for the project is developed to determine financial feasibility.

Development

Initial budgets are approved/funded. A Land Plan is developed and managed through the local government zoning and ordinance approval processes. The engineering, submission, and municipal approval of final plans occurs. A General Contractor is engaged and managed through the construction phase of the project.

Sales

We are experienced in selecting and managing onsite marketing/sales staff to work with potential homeowners in a "client-oriented" approach as they make such an important decision for their family.
